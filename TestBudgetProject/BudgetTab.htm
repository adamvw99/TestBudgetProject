New to budgeting? Dont worry, we got you covered.

Why Budget?

Budgeting is used to give you full control of your finances. Have you ever wondered where all your
money went? Have you ever felt like you are living from one pay period to the next? You can use a
budget to take control of every dollar and free yourself from worry.

Where to Start.

The best place to start is to figure out where you are. Find the amount that you make every month
so that you have a starting point. Then, find how much your monthly expenses are. Expenses would
include rent, groceries, insurance, utiities/bills, and other guaranteed monthly expenses.

Creating a Budget.

There are many budgeting techniques and strategies to get you started. The key to creating your
budget is to find your goal. Are you budgeting to save more? Are you budgeting to have fun without
the stress? When you know why you are budgeting that will allow you to create a budget that fits your
needs and wants. Below we have provided links that have strategies, tips, and helpful insight to start
budgeting.
