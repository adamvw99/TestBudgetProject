Financial Literacy

Here at BudgetU we are driven to helping you make the best financial decisions so you can feel better about your spending habits while at college. As a college student there are a lot of new expenses you aren't used to. Below are some helpful sites and articles to better understand the position you are in. Staying organized and planning out your expenses is worth the effort. Whether you are preparing ahead for college or currently a student needing some guidance, we're here to help.

Articles and Sites for Becoming More Financially Literate

College Budgeting Guide
This site allows you to customize a ideal college route for you by selecting what kind of degree you are looking for, which field of study you are looking into doing, and what specific major you would like to specify in. From there it recomends schools and allows you to search from there.

College Spending
On this Web page you can find a lot of great information on average college student spending including costs per year for community, public, and private schools. Additionally, information on the smaller details of college spending outside of tuition like transportation and other unique facts that are important to keep in mind.

Affordable College Towns
This site allows similar customization to the first article but allows you to find affordable college towns in the U.S. based on your selected criteria. There are featured programs and social, cultural, and economical factors are all considered.

5 Keys to Financial Literacy
In this Article, students and people eager to learn can be introduced to various financial literacy concepts. The 5 main subjects included are the Basics of Budgeting, Interest Rates, Saving Money, Debt Management, and Account Safety.

Zogo Finance
Zogo is a financial education tool to earn the trust of the modern college student. They work with Banks, Credit Unions and more to teach and reward those who want to improve their financial status and customize their banking to the way they want.
